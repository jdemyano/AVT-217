Brief for Personal Website

Objective

To illustrate work accomplished and provide employers with samples of my work. To show employers motivation and dedication to excellence through works and descriptions of my work's impact.

Audience

The audience of this site varies depending on the rationale for them coming to it.

  • Employers - Reviewing my previous performances and works to determine if I would be a good fit for their company and the open position.
  • Clients - Reviewing my work to see the extent of my abilities and find see how their vision of their project aligns with what I can do.
  • People browsing the internet - Might randomly find my website through their adventures through puppy sketches or video games.

Research

To sell my skills as a producer, most companies do not require a portfolio. In fact, they are primarily interested in using LinkedIn and information on a resume to make hiring decisions. If I would like to showcase how well-rounded I am with game development, then having a portfolio site is one of the best ways to accomplish this.

Content

  • Home page - Contains links to the entire site's navigation system. Introduces my work and highlights my objectives while showcasing my skills.
  • Portfolio - Collection of my best works. Sub pages are for production, 3D animation, and music composition.
  • Resume - Downloadable .pdf document that can be downloaded by employers.
  • About - Includes a summary about who I am and how to contact me.
